About Cindy

Jordan Law’s Legal Assistant Cindy Vance joined our firm in 2014 and is an invaluable asset to our team. Cindy graduated from Lane Community College with an Associate of Applied Science degree in Administrative Assisting and a Career Pathways Certificate in Legal Office Skills. She previously worked as a legal assistant handling Personal Injury, Estate Planning and Administration, and Collections cases. At Jordan Law, P.C., Cindy assists in all areas of the firm’s Juvenile Law practice, a practice area close to her heart as she is the mother of four children.

Cindy maintains close associations with parents, foster families, adoptive families, and the children we represent. Part of representing children involves visiting the children in their homes, and Cindy is responsible for coordinating and often conducting these visits for our youngest clients. Cindy handles all adoption filings and processing and also provides litigation support in all of Susan's practice areas. Before attending Lane Community College, Cindy had many years of customer service experience in the hospitality industry.

She is an avid Ducks fan and enjoys donating her time to the community by volunteering at her children and grandchildren’s sporting events and activities.
