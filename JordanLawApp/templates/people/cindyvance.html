About Cindy

Cindy has been with Jordan Law, P.C. since September, 2014 and is an invaluable asset to our team. Prior to her association with Jordan Law, Cindy graduated from Lane Community College in 2014 with an Associate of Applied Science degree in Administrative Assisting and a Career Pathways Certificate in Legal Office Skills. In 2014, she interned for and was hired by Phillip M. Williams, P.C. as a legal assistant handling Personal Injury, Estate Planning and Administration, and Collections cases.

At Jordan Law, P.C., Cindy assists in all areas of Susan’s Juvenile Law practice, a practice area close to her heart as she is the mother of four children. Cindy is often responsible for visiting our younger clients in their homes and maintains close associations with parents, foster families, and the children we represent. She also provides litigation support in all of Susan’s practice areas and prepares initial petitions for adoption. Before attending Lane Community College, Cindy had many years of customer service experience in the hospitality industry. She is an avid Ducks fan and enjoys donating her time to the community by volunteering at her children and grandchildren’s sporting events and activities.

Lane County Juvenile Law Consortium Member

Susan has been a member of the Lane County Juvenile Law Consortium since 2007. She has served as a defense attorney for Springfield Municipal Court and has also worked as a contract attorney prosecuting cases on behalf of the City of Eugene.

OSBLPRC Member

Susan has served on the Oregon State Bar Local Professional Responsibility Committee, is a long-term member of the Lane County Bar Association and also engages in regular pro bono activities on behalf of low-income clients and in connection with the University of Oregon School of Law.

Felony crime resolution

She won both her first felony criminal trial and her first trial defending a parent in a Termination of Parental Rights trial and has appeared in numerous bench and jury trials in criminal, civil, and juvenile proceedings. Susan continues to grow and expand her practice base with ongoing legal education in her practice areas and membership in the Juvenile and Probate sections of the Oregon State Bar.
