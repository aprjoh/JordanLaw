About April

April graduated, with honors, from Lane Community College with an Associate of Applied Science, Administrative Assistant, paired with a one year Career Pathways Certificate in Legal Office Skills. For nearly 8 years, she has been taking Continuing Legal Education courses through the National Association of Legal Assistants and Oregon Trial Lawyerâ€™s Association, specializing in the following areas: Personal Injury, Probate, Estate Planning, Estate Administration, and Collections. At Jordan Law, P.C. April assists with Probate, Estate Planning, Real Property, Family Law, and Juvenile cases. Before working at Jordan Law, P.C., she was Paralegal and Office Manager at Phillip M. Williams, P.C. for 7 years. Over the course of her career as a Paralegal, April has provided internship opportunities for graduates of the Lane Community College Legal Assistant program, providing on-the-job training and recommendations to help jumpstart their careers.

April is mother to three daughters, born and raised in Springfield, Oregon. She is the Membership Chairperson and Vice President of the Springfield Neighborhood Watch, a cause that is important to her as it strives to create and maintain a sense of community where her family resides.
