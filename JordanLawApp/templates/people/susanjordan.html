About Susan

Susan has been engaged in private practice in Lane County for seventeen years. After graduating from the University of Oregon School of Law in 1997, Susan was admitted to the Oregon State Bar and clerked for three Lane County Circuit Court judges until 2000, when she joined a Springfield firm and practiced in the area of civil litigation, with a focus on real property disputes. Susan was admitted to practice in Federal Court and worked briefly for the University of Oregon School of Law before opening her own practice in 2005, originally Susan Ezzy Jordan, LLC, now Jordan Law, P.C.

Susan’s background includes general civil litigation and exposure to a wide range of practice areas including Civil Litigation, Estate Planning, Family Law and matters involving Real Property. In 2007, she began practicing Juvenile Law and became an active member of the Lane County Juvenile Law Consortium. Juvenile Law constitutes a significant portion of her practice and Susan also continues to assist clients with other issues related to her general practice areas.

Lane County Juvenile Law Consortium Member

Susan has been a member of the Lane County Juvenile Law Consortium since 2007. She has served as a defense attorney for Springfield Municipal Court and has also worked as a contract attorney prosecuting cases on behalf of the City of Eugene.

OSBLPRC Member

Susan has served on the Oregon State Bar Local Professional Responsibility Committee, is a long-term member of the Lane County Bar Association and also engages in regular pro bono activities on behalf of low-income clients and in connection with the University of Oregon School of Law.

Felony crime resolution
