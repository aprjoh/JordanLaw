About Susan

Susan graduated from the Oregon School of Law and was admitted to the Oregon State Bar in 1997. Susan clerked for three Lane County Circuit judges after graduation and joined a Springfield firm in 2000, where she worked in a variety of practice areas including civil litigation, land use, real property litigation and estate planning. Susan opened her own practice in 2005, originally Susan Ezzy Jordan, LLC, now Jordan Law, P.C. She joined the Lane County Juvenile Law Consortium in 2007 and is an active member of that group.

Susan is a current member of the Oregon State Bar, Oregon Criminal Defense Lawyers Association, Lane County Bar Association and the Lane County Juvenile Law Consortium and the Juvenile and Probate sections of the Oregon State Bar Association. She is a past member of Oregon Women Lawyers and the Oregon State Bar Local Professional Responsibility Committee. Susan engages in regular pro bono activities on behalf of low-income clients and in connection with the University of Oregon School of Law. Susan has two children and lives with her husband in Eugene. She enjoys sporting events, being outdoors, and spending time with family, friends, and two dogs, Dave and Bowie.

Lane County Juvenile Law Consortium Member

Susan has been a member of the Lane County Juvenile Law Consortium since 2007 and represents parents and children in all stages of Juvenile Delinquency and Dependency proceedings, including Juvenile Criminal Defense, Juvenile Dependency Practice and Defense, Termination of Parental Rights cases, and Adoptions.

Family Law

Often in connection with our Juvenile Law cases, our firm represents and assists clients with family law cases and issues including dissolutions, legal separations, custody orders, review and drafting of premarital agreements and name and gender changes.

Estate Planning and Administration (Probate)

With the recent addition of our Estate paralegal April Johnson, Probate and Estate administration is a growing practice at our firm. Attorney Susan Ezzy Jordan has experience with guardianship and conservatorship proceedings, probate and small estate administration, estate litigation and basic estate planning and Trusts.

Real Property Litigation and Document Preparation and Drafting

Susan has over twenty years of experience handling real estate and real property matters including adverse possession cases, Covenants, Conditions and Restrictions (CC&R) issues, HOA and construction liens, landlord-tenant disputes and evictions and document drafting and review of easements, earnest money agreements, land sales contracts, real estate contracts, deeds, and other documents related to real property sale, purchase, and ownership.

Adoptions

Susan is a state-certified contractor authorized to prepare and file DHS-sponsored adoptions. Susan also has experience with stepparent adoption and contested adoption proceedings.
